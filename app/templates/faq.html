Knowledge Base (FAQ)

How do I reset my password?

Currently, you must contact your IT administrator to perform a manual password reset in the database.

The database is inaccessible

Check your VPN connection. If you are on the corporate network and still cannot access it, submit a High severity ticket.

How do I create a ticket?

Click "New" in the navbar, fill out the title and description, select the severity level, and submit.

Can I edit a ticket?

Only Administrators can edit or delete tickets. Standard users can only view tickets once submitted.
